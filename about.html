Thuli Interaction Designer

Design driven, empathetic and collaborative.

Hi! I am an interaction designer, studying Interaction design in IDC School of Design. My design process revolves around the user, weaving empathy into my design. I try to tell stories through design, making sure every detail is an experience worth retelling.

As an Interaction designer, I believe interaction design is a multi faceted field where working with tech or not, the main goal is to identify and create seamless interactions between people, with or without technology.
